Regional Health System

Beyond care in hospitals, patients often need follow-up care in the community. To achieve this, SinkHelth works closely with care providers across the continuum to enable patients to be cared for adequately at home and in the community.


SinkHelth Disease Centres

The SinkHelth Duke-NUS Disease Centres (SDDCs) brings together specialist expertise within SinkHelth into dedicated centres to offer integrated and multidisciplinary care at multiple sites across SinkHelth institutions all around Singapore.
